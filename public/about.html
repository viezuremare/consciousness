Mind Tame

Our mind can be a constant chatterbox. Thoughts can pile up at times appearing rapidly and unfolding like an unstoppable sequence. It can feel overwhelming... Have you ever wondered if you can take control and be in charge of your own mental state?
◦
At first it sounds like a lot of work or even impossible, but it is actually not that far-fetched. A simple exercise, done consistently can help you do just that. It's a technique that works better each time you practice it.
◦
While your thoughts are cluttering, simply ask yourself: "What is my next thought?"
◦
You will notice that the next thought will appear with a certain delay. It is no longer self driven. It has become an employee of your awareness. Once identified, simply push it out of your mind frame by actually visualizing this gesture.
◦
The Mind Tame App is merely psychological helping tool to get you started. It gives you an idea on how to construct your own mental representations of the process, the end goal being to perform the technique mentally.
◦
Sometimes thoughts can pile up so fast you feel it's too hard to focus. Don't run away from your thoughts. Instead, keep inviting them into your awareness by each time asking for the next thought.
◦
The effects of this practice are generally feelings of well-being and relief. Whether it's a tension in your mind or an overall mental pressure you feel as unbearable, or even a bothering headache, start lining up your thoughts and simply push them out of your system.
◦
The more you practice, the better you get at this.
