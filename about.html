Mind Tame

Our mind can be a constant chatter-box. Thoughts can pile up at times appearing rapidly and unfolding like an unstoppable sequence. This can feel overwhelming.
◦
Have you ever wondered if you can take control and be in charge of your own mental state?
◦
At first it sounds like a lot of work or even impossible, but it actually is not that far-fetched.
◦
A simple exercise, done consistently can help you do just that. It's a technique that works better each time you practice it.
◦
The objective is to try and clear your mind and then ask yourself:
◦
"What is my next thought?"
◦
You will notice that the next thought will appear with a certain delay. It is no longer self driven. It has become an employee of your awareness.
◦
Once identified, simply swipe it out of your mind frame by actually visualizing this gesture.
◦
That's it.
