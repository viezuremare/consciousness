Mind Tame

Our mind can be a constant chatter-box. Thoughts can pile up at times appearing rapidly and unfolding like an unstoppable sequence. It can feel overwhelming... Have you ever wondered if you can take control and be in charge of your own mental state?
◦
At first it sounds like a lot of work or even impossible, but it is actually not that far-fetched. A simple exercise, done consistently can help you do just that. It's a technique that works better each time you practice it.
◦
The objective is to try and clear your mind and then ask yourself: "What is my next thought?"
◦
You will notice that the next thought will appear with a certain delay. It is no longer self driven. It has become an employee of your awareness. Once identified, simply push it out of your mind frame by actually visualizing this gesture.
◦
The Mind Tame Web App is merely a small psychological helping tool to get you started. By trying out the technique physically, you can get an idea of how to construct your own mental representations of the process.
◦
Sometimes thoughts can pile up so fast you feel it's to hard to focus. Don't run away from your thoughts. Instead keep inviting them into your awareness by asking for the next thought.
◦
The effects of this practice are generally feelings of well being and relief. Whether it's a tension in your mind or a general mental pressure you feel as unbearing, or even a bothering headache, start lining up your thoughts and simply push them out of your system.
◦
The more you practice, the better you get at this.
