Mind Tame

This app is merely a small psychological helping tool to get you started.
By trying out the technique physically, you can get an idea of how to construct your own mental representations of the process.
The idea is to simply identify, lock and swipe your thoughts out of your mind, one by one, thus gaining a sense of control over your own thinking.
Sometimes thoughts can pile up so fast you feel it's to hard to focus. Don't run away from your thoughts. Instead keep inviting them into your awareness by asking for the next thought.
The effects of this practice are generally feelings of well being and relief. Whether it's a tension in your mind or a general mental pressure you feel unbearing, or even a bothering headache, start lining up your thoughts and simply swipe them out of your system.
The more you practice, the better you get at this.
